Bar Chart

Discussion

This horizontal bar chart plots passenger count (cumulative) against airlines within North America (incl. US, Canada, Mexico, Central America). Without filtering the dataset down to North American flights, the data would be excessive for a bar chart visualization.

For this data, I decided to eliminate United Airlines since it dwarved the rest of the data. Small airlines with less than 100,000 flights in 2018 were also eliminated since they did not contribute much to the dataset.
